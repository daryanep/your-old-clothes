Our Story

Gabuts People making some noise.

Your Old Clothes started the way most bands do — badly. A borrowed guitar, a drum kit with a missing snare, and a four-track recorder that hummed louder than the vocals. Three years later, the hum became a sound, and the sound became a band.

We make warm, nostalgic indie music that sounds like a mixtape someone left in a jacket pocket. Analog synths, live drums, tape saturation, and lyrics about things you forgot you remembered.
